Black Switch

  • Feels heavy, but with no noise nor feedback
  • Recommended for accuracy

Blue Switch

  • Known to be 'clicky'
  • Audible clicking noise when typed

Brown Switch

  • The most popular switch
  • Gives feedback when typed, but with no noise
  • Recommended for first-time switch buyers

Green Switch

  • Known to be 'clicky'
  • Feels heavy
  • Recommended for accuracy

Red Switch

  • Easy to press
  • Makes no noise
  • Gives no feedback

White Switch

  • Gives audible feedback
  • Soft clicking noise

Yellow Switch

  • The quickest keyboard switch
  • Recommended for gaming
